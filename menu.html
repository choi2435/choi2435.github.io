specialty

STRAWBERRY JASMINE TEA FRESCA

A refreshing combination of strawberries blended with our jasmine tea. No milk added. Part of our “Tea Fresca” series.

STRAWBERRY CALI-PICO

Our version of the beloved Calpico drink. Real strawberries blended with our jasmine tea and low-fat Kefir yogurt.

STRAWBERRY RICE MILK

Our housemade strawberry purée poured over dairy-free rice milk. A tasty treat for kids and the kid in you.

HORCHATA

Our signature drink and our nod to the Mission (where it all started)! House- made with rice milk, cinnamon and sugar. Splash of organic milk for taste and mouthfeel.

Contains dairy.

DIRTY HORCHATA

Our housemade horchata with a next-level twist: we’ve layered a double shot of our own custom house blend of espresso over the top. Contains dairy.

CHOCOCHATA

Our take on the Mexican champurrado. Organic premium drinking chocolate over rice milk and cinnamon, with a splash of organic milk for mouthfeel. Contains dairy.

THAI TEA

Brewed from a strong Ceylon tea combined with Straus Family organic milk and organic condensed milk. Can be made with or without dairy.

HONG KONG STYLE

The same black tea base as our classic milk tea, but we use organic condensed milk. Also known for a stronger tea taste. Contains dairy.

BLACK SESAME LATTE

Housemade black sesame ginger paste topped over a creamy bed of milk. Delicious AF, if you ask us. Contains dairy and nuts.
